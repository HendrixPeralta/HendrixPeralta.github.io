Hello, my name is Hendrix, a master student with a passion for Data Science and Data Analytics. My current research focuses on regional development, spatial econometrics, satellite data, the Sustainable Development Index, and machine learning.

Although during my bachelor degree I worked with production productivity and automation. It was not until years after that I understood the importance of data driven decision making. My experience supervising maintenance work allowed me to use data as a tool to improve the efficiency of the maintenance work done by the department reducing production costs. Additionally, a deep understanding and monitoring of maintenance trends and the use of machine different parts gave us the oportunity to mantain a lean inventory stock.

Coupled with that, my experience as a team member in diverse projects also showed me the importance of data and its understanding in the economic side of those projects. These experiences carved in me a passion for interpreting the data led me to decide the course of my post graduate studies towards data analytics and data science.
